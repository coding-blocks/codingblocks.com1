SUCCESS STORY - CODING BLOCKS NOIDA
In the last couple of months, we told you about our new centre coming up in Sector 2, Noida. Over the years, we got innumerable requests and complains regarding travel times from Noida, Ghaziabad and even East Delhi, so we thought it's about time we open a center in Noida.
 
It gives us great pride in announcing that the Noida centre has broken all previous records of class occupancy for a new centre, and by a county mile. We were planning on running single instances of most batches, but the sheer amount of requests made us run two instances of almost every batch. Courses like Java, C++, Android Development, Web Development, Machine Learning and Python with App Development are running to overflowing classes, and some really good reviews. We would like to take this opportunity to thank each one of our students, who joined us, and everyone else who helped us spread a word about the new centre. The Noida centre will continue to run multiple weekend batches starting this August as well, so register now on the aforementioned link.

iOS WORKSHOP @ CODING BLOCKS NOIDA
Early last month, Coding Blocks conducted its first ever workshop on iOS Development. Held at the Noida centre, the workshop was ocnducted by experienced iOS developer, Tushar Jindal. The informative workshop helped enthusiastic students explore and understand topics such as Xcode, basic UI building, UI elements, MVC pattern of iOS Apps, navigation controller, delegation, protocol, integration of third party libraries using Cocoapods and Google Maps API in merely two days. The workshop was enjoyed a lot by all students, as they learned a lot and built their own functional apps by the end of the workshop.
