Team Coding Blocks has always believed in the philosophy of changing with the evolving times, and we continue to do so. With the new year, we hope to serve you with a new energy and a new enthusiasm. We aim to bring newer courses on the latest technologies, and newer ways to learn. CODING BLOCKS FELICITATED
The collective efforts of team Coding Blocks were awarded this January at the Sixth Higher Education Conclave organized by Indian Education Network and What After College. Coding Blocks was awarded the second prize under the category of Top 7 Summer Training Programs for Students in Delhi and NCR. As the youngest institute there in terms of years of being active, it gives us great pride to be felicitated with such an honour.
 
At the same event, Coding Blocks founding member Arnav Gupta was invited to participate in a panel discussion titled Jobs vs Higher Education vs Entrepreneurship. He spoke about his own experiences and also about the philosophy that Coding Blocks imparts to its students. The panel also had other emminent professionals from the education industry, such as Mr. Arun Sharma from Mindworkzz CAT Classes and Mr. Rajeev Saumitra from Paramount Classes among others.

CODING BLOCKS CONDUCTS COLLEGE WORKSHOPS
ANDROID DEV WS @ MAIT
Team Coding Blocks conducted a workhop on Introductory Android Development at Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology on January 25. The workshop was conducted by CB founding member Arnav Gupta. Team Coding Blocks conducted a workhop on Basic Open Source at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women on January 30.
